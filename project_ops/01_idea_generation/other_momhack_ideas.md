### 1. "Recall" - The Context-Aware Meeting Copilot
**Track:** AI Second Brain
**The Pitch:** A background daemon that listens to your Zoom/Meet calls and proactively "whispers" relevant context to you.
**Why It Wins:** High utility, real-time audio + vector search, agentic behavior.
**Tech Stack:** Python, OpenAI Whisper, Qdrant, Tauri (Overlay UI).

### 2. "Guardian" - The Contextual Compliance Shield
**Track:** Unstructured Data RAG
**The Pitch:** Watches what you write (Email, Slack, Docs) and warns in real-time if you contradict past legal agreements or policies.
**Why It Wins:** Enterprise-grade problem, privacy-focused, low hallucination tolerance.
**Tech Stack:** Electron, Qdrant (Hybrid Search), Local LLM (Ollama).
